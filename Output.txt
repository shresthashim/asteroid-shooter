=== ASTEROID SHOOTER ===
Controls:
  Arrow Keys - Steer and thrust
  UP - Thrust forward
  DOWN - Brake/slow down
  LEFT/RIGHT - Rotate
  Spacebar - Shoot
  R - Restart game
  N - Next level (when level complete)

Objective: Destroy all asteroids!
Large asteroids break into smaller ones.
Avoid colliding with asteroids!
